Privacy Policy for Trackaroo

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Trackaroo is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

We may collect personal details such as your name, email, phone number, and location data to provide accurate bus tracking and notifications.

Data is used to display real-time bus locations, manage routes, improve safety, and provide notifications for pickup and drop timings.

Data Security

We use secure servers and encryption to protect your data. Your personal information will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

You can request data deletion or account removal anytime through our Delete Account page.
